I PUSH, PRACTISE, AND EXERCISE



I also love to run and strum (the ukulele). They’re both new loves in my life and I can’t get enough of them. I’ve always found physical challenge as rewarding as the creative kind and pushing my mind and body ultimately brings out the best in both. I’m grateful to be happy, healthy, and humble and being mindful, minimal, and meanginful plays a large role in my life too. I run regularly, drink rarely, drug never, fast intermittently, and Wim Hof daily. All of the above keep me balanced — mostly sane and mostly happy — and make me a healthy, reliable human…



tl;dw — Wimm Hof is redefining the science of human physiology through the power of his mind. It’s almost unbelievable (and I don’t use adverbs lightly). Part of Wim’s daily routine is cold water exposure. It’s also part of mine.


When I’m not running, strumming, or shivering Iceman-style I can be found reading, writing, solving a Rubik’s Cube, or streaming a podcast or two. I love to learn. I’m currently learning Sign Language, a language like every other — one that connects people — and I hope to connect to more people speaking it.
